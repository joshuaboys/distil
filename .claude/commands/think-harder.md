# Think Harder

Take a step back and think more deeply about this problem.

## Your Task

$ARGUMENTS

## Instructions

1. **Pause and Reflect**: Don't jump to solutions immediately
2. **Question Assumptions**: What are we taking for granted?
3. **Consider Alternatives**: What other approaches exist?
4. **Identify Risks**: What could go wrong?
5. **Think Systematically**: Break down the problem

## Analysis Framework

### Problem Understanding
- What exactly is being asked?
- What are the constraints?
- What does success look like?

### Root Cause Analysis
- Why does this problem exist?
- What are the underlying factors?
- Is this a symptom of a deeper issue?

### Solution Space
- What are all possible approaches?
- What are the trade-offs of each?
- Which approach best fits the constraints?

### Implementation Considerations
- What are the dependencies?
- What could block progress?
- How will we validate success?

Take your time. Quality of thought matters more than speed.
